The brand known worldwide for their high-end kitchen equipment, wants to increase their customer experience to allow their customers to take their food to the next level by offering different nutrition and recipe programs. In order to establish the brand's positioning, to enable their customers to improve their quality of life and cooking experience and to retain existing customers and acquire new ones. I made this work at my previous agency, Emakina.

I help them to

• Furnish insights for the french market
• Highlight opportunities
• Recommandations for short, medium & long term.

Challenge

How to elevate nutrition at a next level with programs & recipes ?

Insight

Before even selecting and equipping the material, people first start by looking for a specific recipe, and then, the used setup. Not the other way around.

So how do you promote cooking equipment through content, at what level, with what format and where is the best place to do it?

My Work

Project led by Emakina NL, they wanted to know, understand and identify the specificities in terms of content around recipes and nutrition programs on the French market. In order to seize opportunities and uses. That's why they came to me. My role is to benchmark the market and to seize the axes of positioning to help KitchenAid to take part on this market. After an in-depth research, I delivered them insights and recommendations to be implemented in the short, medium and long term.

Results / Deliverables

• Recipes Content Market Benchmark
• +10 Opportunities Deck for french market
• Over 70% engagement on social networks by 2022
• More than 30 countries covered
• This work has contributed to a 27% increase in e-commerce sales.
